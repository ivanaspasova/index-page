About Me

My name is Ivana Spasova. I am currently studying Web Development at Northwestern Coding Boot Camp. I love building web pages and develop things that can be used by other people. I like the fact that web developers have the power to change how people live just with a single invention. The world has never been more connected thanks to the World Wide Web. People building websites can contribute to the future of our generation. I have always wanted to see what is it like "behind the scenes" of these great websites. Now I understand how it's done and I love the fact that someday I can also contribute to the development of our digital world.
